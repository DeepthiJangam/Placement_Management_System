Welcome......

Welcome to the Placement Management System (PMS)! This system is a powerful tool designed to streamline and simplify the management of student placements, internships, and career opportunities within educational institutions, career centers, or organizations.

Purpose of the Placement Managment System

The primary goal of the PMS is to efficiently connect students seeking placement opportunities with employers offering those opportunities. It serves as a centralized platform for various stakeholders, including students, faculty advisors, and employers, to interact and collaborate seamlessly throughout the placement process.

How to Use This Guide

This User Guide is organized to provide you with step-by-step instructions and insights on how to navigate and utilize the PMS effectively. It covers various aspects of the system, from creating an account and applying for placements to communication tools, progress tracking, and more.
If you ever encounter any questions or need assistance, this guide is your go-to resource. We have designed it to be clear and comprehensive, with detailed instructions and helpful tips to ensure a smooth experience.

Key Features and Benefits

1. Placement Opportunities :
Access a wide range of placement opportunities posted by Admin.

2. Placement Progress Tracking :
Stay informed about the status of your placements.

3.Feedback :
User can be able to provide feedback to the admin regarding the improvement of the placements, and about UI etc....

4.User Authentication :
Only Authorized users can access the portal. The new users has to register to the portal by filling required data. The existing users can directly login to the portal and has access to use it.

User Roles

User Roles The PMS caters to different user roles, including:
Students: Seek placements and manage applications.
Admin: Oversee and manage the overall system, including user management and configuration. Verify and add trusted companies and add jobs provided by companies.
